Look for bots

We have the feeling that we might have cheaters in our system. So we would like you to find the cheaters. First it might be a good idea to think about how we might detect bots/cheaters (what would an incentive be for a cheater?).

When you have some ideas, try to write a projection to detect the bots.

Spoilers

Here are some ways to detect bots:

  • they always answer correctly
  • they answer super fast
  • they play too many games
